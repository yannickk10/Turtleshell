TurtleShell was designed with simplicity and ease of use in mind to help users stream their favourite T.V. programmes and films. The app links to the media libraries of popular streaming services available in Ireland, including Netflix, Sky Go, Amazon Prime Video and many more.

Overview

TurtleShell has partnered with streaming services across Ireland to bring you the most seamless and comprehensive experience possible. Our app allows you to pick and choose whichever services you're subscribed to and log into their accounts to add them to your media library. This negates the need for switching between apps every time you want to watch a show from a different service.

The app works by communicating directly with the streaming source server so you watch at the highest available quality your internet connection supports. This also means you'll be up to date whenever new shows are added by the services you're subscribed to. See below for the full list of services we currently support.

Features

The following are some of the stand-out features of TurtleShell

➤ Customizable user-friendly interface

With our advanced theming engine, you can change the UI to what fits you the most.

➤ Advanced content filtering

Our filtering engine provides a myriad of options that help you find excatly the type of content you're looking for.

➤ Free

The app itself is completely free and is supported by unintrusive banner ads that are displayed outside of shows/programmes. You only pay to the services you're subscribed to.

Word from the creators

We developed TurtleShell to cater for a gap in the streaming industry, in which there was nothing that unified the streaming services one could be subscribed to. There was also the lack of a good search algorithm and content filtering options in existing services that made it more difficult to find the right show. TurtleShell tackles both these issues to provide the perfect gateway to your favourite shows and films.
